# InvTrack: Innovation Strategy Summary

> **TL;DR**: Build Portfolio Health Score (4 weeks) → Achieve category leadership as "Fitbit for Money"

---

## 🎯 The Big Bet: Portfolio Health Score™

**Vision**: Make portfolio health as tangible as physical health with a unified score (0-100).

**Why This Wins**:
- ✅ **Highest RICE score** (68 - far ahead of other ideas)
- ✅ **Creates habit**: Users check daily (like Fitbit steps)
- ✅ **Viral potential**: "My portfolio health is 82, what's yours?"
- ✅ **Monetization driver**: Improving score requires premium features
- ✅ **Moat builder**: Network effects + data moat
- ✅ **Category defining**: No competitor has this

**The Pitch**:
> "InvTrack gives you a **Portfolio Health Score** - a single number that tells you if your money is truly working for you. Like a Fitbit for your finances, it alerts you to risks, suggests improvements, and keeps you on track to your goals."

## 🗓️ Implementation Roadmap

### **Q2 2026 - Foundation** (Apr-Jun)

**Week 1-4: Portfolio Health Score MVP**
- Scoring algorithm (Returns 30%, Diversification 25%, Liquidity 20%, Goals 15%, Actions 10%)
- Dashboard widget (circular progress, color-coded)
- Historical tracking (weekly snapshots)
- **Target**: 70%+ MAU view score, DAU/MAU +10%

**Week 5-8: Predictive Alerts (Rule-Based)**
- 3 free alerts (maturity, concentration, underperformance)
- 12 premium alerts (renewal cliff, goal derailment, tax opportunities, etc.)
- **Target**: 50%+ act on alerts, +2% premium conversion

### **Q3 2026 - Intelligence** (Jul-Sep)

**Week 1-6: Smart AI Assistant**
- GPT-4/Gemini integration
- 10 Q/month free, unlimited premium
- Proactive weekly insights
- **Target**: 40%+ MAU use chat, +1% conversion

**Week 7-12: Peer Benchmarking**
- Percentile ranking (XIRR, allocation, Health Score)
- Category benchmarks (by type, platform)
- Community best practices
- **Target**: 60%+ MAU view, network effects start

### **Q4 2026 - Monetization** (Oct-Dec)

**Week 1-8: Alternative Marketplace**
- Platform directory (P2P, FD, Bonds, Real Estate)
- Rate comparison engine
- Referral tracking (₹500-2000/lead)
- **Target**: ₹25L ARR from referrals

**Week 9-20: Tax Optimization**
- Tax-loss harvesting

- **Target**: +2% premium conversion

### **2027+ - Scale**
