📈 SMART METRICS

XIRR - Your True Annual Return
Most apps show basic percentage returns. XIRR accounts for WHEN you invested and WHEN you got money back. This is what banks and professionals use.

MOIC - Your Money Multiple
Invested ₹1 lakh and got ₹1.5 lakh back? That's 1.5x MOIC. Simple way to see how much your money multiplied.

🔥 FIRE NUMBER - PLAN YOUR FINANCIAL INDEPENDENCE

Know exactly how much you need to retire early and live off your investments forever.

FIRE = Financial Independence, Retire Early. Enter your monthly expenses and target retirement age. We calculate your FIRE Number using the proven 4% Safe Withdrawal Rate (25x your annual expenses, inflation-adjusted for India).

Track your progress automatically:
• Visual milestones showing your journey
• Coast FIRE calculator (when you can stop saving)
• Projected retirement date based on current pace
• Works with ALL your investments

Example: ₹50K monthly expenses → ₹3.6 Cr FIRE Number at age 45
