Why InvTracker?

Traditional portfolio trackers focus on stocks and mutual funds. InvTracker is built specifically for alternative investments where cash flows happen at irregular intervals.

XIRR (Extended Internal Rate of Return) is the gold standard for measuring returns on investments with irregular cash flows - exactly what you need for P2P lending, real estate, or any investment where you invest and receive money at different times.
