📈 SMART METRICS

XIRR - Your True Annual Return
Most apps show basic percentage returns. XIRR accounts for WHEN you invested and WHEN you got money back. This is what banks and professionals use.

MOIC - Your Money Multiple
Invested ₹1 lakh and got ₹1.5 lakh back? That's 1.5x MOIC. Simple way to see how much your money multiplied.
